Game Consoles

1. PlayStation

The PlayStation is a popular gaming console developed by Sony. It offers a wide range of exclusive games and powerful hardware.

Key Features:

  • PlayStation 5: Released in 2020.
  • Supports 4K and 8K video.
  • Exclusive titles like Demon's Souls and Spider-Man: Miles Morales.
  • Ray-tracing and fast SSD storage.

2. Xbox

The Xbox is a gaming console developed by Microsoft, known for services like Xbox Game Pass.

Key Features:

  • Xbox Series X: Released in 2020.
  • Supports 4K gaming at 60 fps.
  • Integration with Game Pass, offering access to hundreds of games.
